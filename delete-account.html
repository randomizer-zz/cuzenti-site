Account Management

Delete Account

Cuzenti LLC  ·  Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request deletion of your Cuzenti account and the personal data associated with it. Follow the steps below to submit your request. We will process all deletion requests within 30 days.

How to Request Deletion

01

Delete from within the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the Cuzenti app. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will immediately remove your account and queue your data for deletion.

02

Or submit a request by email

If you no longer have access to the app, send an email to support@cuzenti.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

03

We verify and confirm

We may send a verification email to confirm your identity before processing the request. Once verified, we will confirm deletion and process it within 30 days.

04

What happens after deletion

Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered. Your username will become available again. See the table below for exactly what data is deleted and what, if anything, is retained.

What Gets Deleted & What Is Retained

Data Type Action Retention Period
Email address & account credentials Deleted Immediately upon processing
Display name / username Deleted Immediately upon processing
Game progress & quest data Deleted Within 30 days
Match history & ELO rating Deleted Within 30 days
Purchase & subscription records Retained 7 years (legal / tax obligation)
Anonymized aggregate gameplay statistics Retained Indefinitely (not linked to you)
Crash logs & error reports Deleted Within 30 days
