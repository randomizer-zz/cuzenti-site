About Us

A mobile entertainment studio built around original ideas.

Cuzenti LLC is a Connecticut-based limited liability company focused on the development and publishing of original mobile entertainment products for iOS and Android platforms.

We are currently in active development of our debut title — a mobile application built around an original gameplay concept. Our work is guided by a commitment to originality, craft, and the belief that the best interactive experiences are ones that feel genuinely new.

Cuzenti operates as an independent studio, with full ownership of its intellectual property and creative direction.
